Our core values

JETS Club's core values unite us across geographies, teams and levels. They are our shared understanding of how we work at JETS Club - how we make decisions, how we collaborate, how we treat each other and how we think about the value we are creating for our community of students and teachers

STAY
CURIOUS

Always wonder. Always explore.

  • We approach every question with a sense of wonder, and we do it with humility and respect
  • We talk about what went wrong and freely share lessons learned with others
  • We embrace the unexpected and look for ways to see change as opportunity
  • We challenge the status quo and always seek to better ourselves, our work and our team

STRIVE FOR
BETTER

Reach far. Aim high. Dream big.

  • We go above and beyond and aim higher than what's required
  • We aren't afraid to fail, and when we do, we course-correct and learn from our mistakes
  • We are passionate about Brainly's mission and our role in it
  • We foster an environment of trust, respect and inclusion/li>

LEND
A HAND

We're all in this together.

  • We ask for help when we need it and we offer help always
  • We approach everything and everyone with a focus on collaboration
  • We act with the success of the team in mind
  • We all know something we can teach others

DELIVER
WITH
QUALITY

Move fast. Do it right. Be proud.

  • We set ambitious goals and commit to them
  • We take risks that lead to innovation
  • We deliver results with quality and on time
  • We take full responsibility for and ownership of our results

LEAD HIGH-

Inspire. Develop. Engage. Deliver.

  • Lead by example
  • Be an empathetic leader and connect with your team
  • Help people grow
  • Translate company vision and strategy into team goals and make them happen
